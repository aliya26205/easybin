About EasyBin

The idea for EasyBin originated from observing public spaces where waste bins often overflow due to the lack of timely information and are sometimes difficult to locate, causing delays in waste collection. At the same time, housekeeping staff spend valuable time manually checking bins that may not require immediate attention, leading to inefficiency and unhygienic conditions.
